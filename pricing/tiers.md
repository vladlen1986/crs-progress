# CRS Pricing Tiers

Authoritative pricing reference. All prices in USD. Annual contracts only, 
paid upfront. Setup fee is one-time, 100% upfront, non-refundable after kickoff.

> **Currency note:** USD pricing applies to US, Canadian, Caribbean, Macau, 
> and LATAM prospects. For European customers, quote in EUR (separate sheet 
> when needed).

## Setup Fee Tiers (one-time)

| Tier | Setup fee | Included |
|---|---:|---|
| **Basic** | $2,750 | Tenant provisioning, role config, 1 admin training (2h), CSV import (employees + subjects), go-live checklist |
| **Standard** (recommended default) | $3,800 | Basic + 2 training sessions (admin + end-user), workflow customization (up to 5 tweaks), 30 days hypercare |
| **Premium** | $7,000 | Standard + extended onboarding, custom report templates, 1 integration assist, 60 days hypercare |

**Hard floor: $1,600.** Below that, the setup fee doesn't cover labor.

### Setup fee math (Standard, $3,800)

- Kickoff + scoping call: 2h
- Tenant setup, branding, roles/permissions config: 6h
- Data import (employees, subjects, locations): 4h
- Admin training + recording: 3h
- End-user training: 2h
- Documentation + handover: 2h
- Hypercare buffer (30 days, ~1h/week): 5h
- Total: ~24h × $155/h blended = $3,720 → rounded to $3,800

The minimum bundle that delivers a complete daily workflow.

1. Users & Auth
2. Roles & Permissions
3. Surveillance Reporting
4. Incident Management (escalation from reports)

6. Daily Activity Log + End of Shift Report (handover)

12. Visitor / Contractor Log

**Pro tier also includes these capabilities (not module-counted):**
- Operational Alerts (software-only)

Pro + remaining ~25 modules across HR/Employees, Compliance, Communication, 
and full Admin/Core.

Includes:

- Internal Comms, Comments & Mentions, Notifications Center
- Cross-property reporting (multi-site rollup)
- API access + webhooks
- SSO (SAML/OIDC)

- SLA: 4h response, 99.9% uptime
- Floor Alert Stations (hardware add-on — see decisions.md)

## Hard Rules

- **Annual contracts only.** Casino procurement is annual. Monthly billing 
  kills cash flow and invites churn.
- **Setup fee always.** Onboarding takes 20–40 hours. Free setup = unprofitable 
  customers + cheap perception.
- **Per-property pricing**, not per-user. Industry standard. Caps confusion 
  and prevents customers gaming seat counts.
- **Property definition** must be in the contract: "A casino with 2 floors 
  in one building = 1 property. Two casinos owned by the same group in 
  different cities = 2 properties."
- **Setup fee 100% upfront**, before any work. Non-negotiable.
- **Setup fee non-refundable** once kickoff call happens. State in SOW.
- **Discount the annual to win deals — never the setup fee.** Setup is the 
  protection against unprofitable onboarding.
- **Don't sell Enterprise in year 1** unless a 5+ property prospect with 
  budget asks. Cannot support solo. List on pricing page to anchor Pro as 
  the reasonable middle option.

## Pilot Pricing

The first casino (pilot) does **not** pay setup fee. Trade is:

- 3–6 month free pilot
- Auto-converts to paid annual at locked rate (lock at start)
- Logo + name usage rights
- Written case study at month 4
- 2 reference calls with prospects
- Bi-weekly 30-min feedback sessions
- Named champion on customer side
- Exit clause: if no conversion, vendor keeps all IP and learnings

If the pilot pushes to pay something, label it "data migration & training fee" 
and cap at $1,500–$2,000.

## Sanity Test (apply to every deal)

At the proposed Core price, can the deal afford:

- 6h sales
- 30h onboarding
- 2h/mo support × 12 = 24h
- Bubble WU costs at expected usage
- ≥60% margin

If no → too low. Walk or raise.
